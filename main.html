Goa


Goa, a tiny emerald on the west coast of India, with its natural Scenic beauty, abundant greenery, attractive beaches, historical temples and churches, colourful feasts and festivals, and above all warm and hospitable people with a rich cultural milieu, has today emerged as an ideal tourist destination worldwide.

  1. Uluwatu Temple: A stunning sea temple located on a cliff.
  2. Tegallalang Rice Terraces: Famous for its beautiful scenes of rice paddies.
  3. Mount Batur: An active volcano offering scenic views and hiking opportunities.
  4. Seminyak: A trendy area known for its luxury hotels, restaurants, and shops.
  5. Ubud Monkey Forest: A natural reserve and temple complex home to hundreds of monkeys.
  • Nasi Goreng: A popular Indonesian fried rice dish.
  • Mie Goreng: Indonesian fried noodles often served with vegetables and meat.
  • Babi Guling: A traditional Balinese roasted pork dish.
  • Satay: Grilled meat skewers served with a spicy peanut sauce.
  • Lawar: A traditional mix containing fine chopped meat, vegetables, grated coconut, and spices.
